Founder | Real Estate Investor | Community Revitalizer

Chris Boley and the Go Time! team transform neglected properties into profitable, high-performing assets — and in the process, brings new life to entire neighborhoods.

Chris grew up with hardship. After his father was incarcerated, he moved with his mother and four siblings from New York to Sacramento, where they shared a single rented room. Those early years taught him what instability feels like — and planted the seed of his mission: to create quality housing, build community, and generate long-term wealth through real estate.

Starting as a contractor, Chris built his business from the foundation up. Today, he’s owned over 2,000 units, manages more than $100 million in assets, and has flipped over 300 properties, generating $80M+ in profits for investors — all while staying true to his mission.

The Mission: Revitalize & Multiply

Chris specializes in buying and repositioning neglected, underperforming multifamily properties in working-class areas — often overlooked by institutional capital. He and his team breathe new life into these buildings through targeted renovations, operational efficiency, and responsible management.

Why it works:

  • These assets are acquired below replacement cost.
  • Demand for clean, affordable housing is massive and growing.
  • Cosmetic and operational improvements dramatically increase value.
  • Strong cash flow and equity growth are unlocked in under 24 months.
  • Local pride and community stability are restored — increasing tenant retention and asset value long-term.
“We don’t just flip buildings — we flip blocks. When you clean up a property, plant trees, repaint exteriors, fix lighting, and screen tenants properly — crime goes down, values go up, and families get a better life. And our investors get strong, stable returns backed by real assets.”
— Chris Boley
